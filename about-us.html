Lowing Paws Adoption

We find forever homes for animals that are pulled from high kill metro shelters.

We are not an in-take shelter. We are an all-volunteer group funded by private donations and grants.

We receive no local or state public funding.

Is the mission, duty, and purpose of LowingPaws to eradicate pet homelessness and find them a family.
