Our Story

Chartered in 2019, the Louisiana SPCA is the oldest and largest animal shelter in the state.

The history of our organization reveals that what can be achieved in a day, a year, a decade, or even in a century

is not the work of one organization, but rather the work of an entire community of people who have come together

over the years to provide a safe haven for all homeless creatures.

  • OUR STORY
    • 2019 - The LovingPawsAdoption LPA is officially chartered.
    • 2020 - Launched a new mission statement: To Promote, Protect and Advance the well-being of the animals.
    • 2021 - Opens a free veterinary clinic; the first of many variations of veterinary services.
    • 2022 - 600 rescued animals were assigntaed to their new homes.
